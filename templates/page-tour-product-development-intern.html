Job Description

As a Tour Product Development Intern, you will assist the product team in researching, designing, and refining tour itineraries and packages. You will gain hands-on exposure to the end-to-end process of creating a market-ready tour ‚Äì from idea generation and competitive analysis to itinerary planning and cost marketing preparation.

Key Responsibilities

  • Market & Competitor Research: Identify emerging travel trends, compare competing tour packages, and analyze customer reviews.
  • Itinerary Development: Draft creative and cost-effective itineraries, including transportation, accommodation, and dining.
  • Supplier Coordination: Communicate with hotels, local guides, and transportation providers to gather quotations and verify service details.
  • Content Creation Support: Assist with copywriting for product descriptions, promotional materials, and social media captions.
  • Field Testing (Optional but Encouraged): Join trial tours, providing feedback on logistics, guest experience, and safety.
  • Data & Budget Management: Track expenses, analyze pricing models, and prepare simple cost breakdowns.

Requirements

  • Current student or recent graduate in Tourism, Hospitality, Business, or Communications (or related field).
  • Strong research and writing skills with an eye for creative storytelling.
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Office or Google Workspace (Excel/Sheets for budgeting).
  • Familiarity with Canva/Photoshop is a plus for creating visual mockups.
  • Enthusiastic traveler with a passion for discovering new destinations.
